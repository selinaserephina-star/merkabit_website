What is the Merkabit Framework?

A geometric architecture
that derives physics
from a single closed unit.

The Merkabit is a ternary computational unit on the Eisenstein lattice — the dual-tetrahedron projection of a closed coherent cycle. From it, a single architecture yields the fine structure constant, the cosmological constant, Newton's gravitational constant, the Standard Model gauge group, three fermion generations, and ten more constants of physics.

Unifying invariantPSL(2,7), the projective special linear group of order 168.
SubstrateThe Eisenstein lattice — the natural triangular tiling of the plane.
Hardware5 of 5 pre-registered predictions confirmed on IBM Eagle r3.
FalsifiabilityEvery claim attaches to a named, public threshold that would refute it.
Fifteen constants. Zero parameters. One geometry.

The framework was built by Selina Stenberg — a mystic, business owner, and deep meditator without formal physics training — in collaboration with an AI partner over a year of dialogue. Hardware verification is led by CTO Thor Henning Hetland on IBM Quantum machines.

The site exists to make the work directly available to anyone curious enough to read it, without requiring institutional gatekeepers. Read the science. Walk the journey. Decide for yourself.
