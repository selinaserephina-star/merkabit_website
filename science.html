Pillar 2 · Unifying Invariant

PSL(2,7) — order 168

The projective special linear group of order 168 — the symmetry group of the Fano plane — is the single invariant unifying α, the Riemann zeros, the Klein quartic, the Yang–Mills mass gap, and matter-antimatter asymmetry.
